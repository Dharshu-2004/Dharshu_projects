About Dr. Vijay Harikrishnan

Consultant Neonatologist and Paediatrician with 14+ years of experience. He completed his MBBS from Rajamuthiah Medical College, Annamalai University, and holds FRCPCH and MRCPCH from London. He also completed a PG Diploma in Child Health from the UK. Dr. Harikrishnan's expertise spans paediatric and neonatal care, specializing in emergency resuscitation and intensive care management.
